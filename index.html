We've Helped Thousands Of People

Whether you're looking for help with anxiety, depression, grief, relationship challenges, or other, at the Feeling Good Institute, you'll work with a therapist who specializes in helping others like you. Our therapists have all had hundreds of hours of training in effective therapy and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) .
